The Sunday Sunrise Liturgy

David Noeth opens the show the same way every Sunday. Almost.

Five Sunday mornings, recorded off the air. David Noeth's opening is a small liturgy — the same words, the same cadence, week after week. But a handful of things change every time: the clock, the date, the sunrise and sunset, how much the day has grown or shrunk, and the musical he's featuring. This is that ritual, laid bare. Pick a Sunday and press play — the script fills in and follows along.
